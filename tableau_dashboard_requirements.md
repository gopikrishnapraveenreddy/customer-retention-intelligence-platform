# Tableau Dashboard Requirements

## Dashboard Title
Customer Churn Risk Analysis & Retention Strategy Dashboard

## KPI Cards
- Total Customers
- Churned Customers
- Churn Rate
- Average Monthly Charges

## Recommended Visuals

### 1. Churn Distribution
Chart Type: Donut Chart  
Dimension: Churn  
Measure: Count of CustomerID

### 2. Churn by Contract Type
Chart Type: Bar Chart  
Dimension: Contract  
Measure: Churn Rate or Churned Customers

### 3. Churn by Internet Service
Chart Type: Bar Chart  
Dimension: InternetService  
Measure: Churn Rate or Churned Customers

### 4. Churn by Payment Method
Chart Type: Bar Chart  
Dimension: PaymentMethod  
Measure: Churn Rate or Churned Customers

### 5. Churn by Tenure Group
Chart Type: Bar Chart  
Dimension: Tenure Group  
Measure: Churn Rate

### 6. Monthly Charges by Churn Status
Chart Type: Column Chart  
Dimension: Churn  
Measure: Average Monthly Charges

## Suggested Filters
- Gender
- Contract
- InternetService
- PaymentMethod
- Tenure Group
